Need help?

  1. Step 1

    Go to the menu and click on "Identify"

  2. Step 2

    Click on the camera icon and take or upload a picture of the place

  3. Step 3

    The platform will identify the condition of the water and classify it in one of the 3 categories

  4. Step 4

    Tips and actions to take will be recommended according to the situation

  5. Step 5

    Questions and answers from the forum will be also suggested to you

  6. Step 6

    Contact nearby volunteers to help you act and share your experience!
